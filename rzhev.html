Battles of Rzhev

Date

Jan 1942 – Mar 1943

Location

Rzhev Salient, near Moscow

Result

German Tactical Defensive Success (Soviet Strategic Cost)

Overview

A series of brutal offensives by the Soviets to eliminate a German salient; heavy losses with limited territorial gain.

Faction Commanders Casualties
USSR Zhukov, Konev ~1,000,000+ casualties
Germany Model ~400,000 casualties

The Conflict

Repeated frontal assaults, difficult terrain, and tenacious German defense turned Rzhev into an attritional slaughter; logistics and winter conditions worsened Soviet losses.

Despite heavy losses, these operations tied down German forces that could not be used elsewhere, indirectly affecting other fronts such as Stalingrad and Kursk.

Significance & Aftermath

  • High Soviet casualties with marginal gains.
  • Tied down significant German forces, impacting other theaters.
