Operations Kutuzov & Rumyantsev

Date

July – August 1943

Location

Orel and Belgorod, USSR

Result

Soviet Victory

Overview

The Soviet counter-offensives launched immediately after the Battle of Kursk.

Faction Commanders Casualties
USSR Rokossovsky, Vatutin ~400,000 casualties
Germany | Von Kluge, Manstein ~200,000 casualties

The Conflict

These operations pushed the exhausted German forces back, liberating key cities like Kharkov (for the final time).

Significance & Aftermath

  • These battles proved that the Red Army could now conduct successful offensives in summer, not just winter, ending German initiative in the East permanently.
