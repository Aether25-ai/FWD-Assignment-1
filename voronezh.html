Battle of Voronezh

Date

June – July 1942

Location

Voronezh, USSR

Result

Axis Tactical Victory / Soviet Strategic Success

Overview

A fierce battle during the German "Case Blue" offensive. While the Germans took the city, the delay fataly slowed their drive to Stalingrad.

Faction Commanders Casualties
USSR Vatutin, Golikov ~370,000 casualties
Germany/Hungary Von Weichs ~95,000 casualties

The Conflict

The city was defended house-to-house, similar to Stalingrad. The Hungarian Second Army was decimated here

Significance & Aftermath

  • The stiff Soviet resistance prevented the Germans from easily enveloping Russian forces, forcing the Germans to stretch their lines too thin toward Stalingrad.
