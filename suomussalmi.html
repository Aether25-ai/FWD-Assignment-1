Battle of Suomussalmi

Date

Dec 1939 – Jan 1940

Location

Suomussalmi, Finland

Result

Decisive Finnish Victory

Overview

Finnish ski troops used mobility and terrain to destroy larger Soviet formations during the Winter War.

Faction Commanders Casualties
Finland Hjalmar Siilasvuo ~750 killed
USSR Vinogradov ~27,000 casualties

The Conflict

Finnish 'motti' tactics divided and isolated Soviet columns; extreme cold, terrain, and Finnish local knowledge produced disproportionate casualties for the Soviets.

The defeats exposed major weaknesses in the Red Army and influenced wartime planning in Germany and elsewhere.

Significance & Aftermath

  • Demonstrated effectiveness of small-unit tactics and mobility in harsh terrain.
  • Humiliated the Red Army and influenced perceptions before Operation Barbarossa.
