The Treaty of Versailles

Many historians argue that the harsh terms imposed on Germany after WWI created the economic instability that allowed the Nazi party to rise to power.

The Invasion of Poland

The immediate cause was the German invasion of Poland on September 1, 1939, which led Britain and France to declare war.
