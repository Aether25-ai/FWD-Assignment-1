Battle of Khalkhin Gol

Date

May – Sept 1939

Location

Mongolia-Manchuria Border

Result

Decisive Soviet Victory

Overview

An undeclared border war between the USSR and Japan that occurred just before WWII officially started in Europe.

Faction Commanders Casualties
USSR Georgy Zhukov ~9,703 killed
Japan Michitarō Komatsubara ~17,000 killed/missing

The Conflict

This was the first major test for Georgy Zhukov. He utilized the first major coordinated mass tank attack in history to encircle the Japanese.

Significance & Aftermath

  • Japan abandoned the "North Strike" doctrine (attacking Russia) and adopted the "South Strike" doctrine (attacking US/UK colonies).
  • This meant the USSR did not have to fight a two-front war.
